# Luravie — Brand Identity Guide (English)

---

## Brand Persona

Luravie is a modern, women-first intimate essentials brand based in Egypt, offering premium bras, panties, lingerie sets, and socks through a seamless online experience — with absolute discretion at its core.

- Elegant, confident, and empowering
- Comfort-first mindset: real quality over fast fashion
- Fluent in both style and substance
- Discreet, thoughtful, and customer-centric

**Tone:** Warm, confident, refined. We speak to women as trusted friends — never preachy, never clinical. We celebrate comfort and self-assurance with grace and clarity.

---

## Mission & Vision

- **Mission:** Empower every woman to feel confident, elegant, and comfortable in her most intimate layers — through premium quality, thoughtful design, and absolute privacy.
- **Vision:** To become Egypt's most trusted and beloved destination for women's intimate essentials, where quality meets discretion and every woman feels seen.

---

## Core Values

1. **Confidence from Within** — True elegance starts with how you feel, not how you look
2. **Uncompromising Quality** — Only the finest fabrics that feel like a second skin
3. **Absolute Discretion** — Your privacy is sacred; plain packaging, no product indicators
4. **Designed for Real Bodies** — Meticulously tested cuts for perfect fit and support
5. **Aesthetic Mindfulness** — Beauty lies in the details, from soft palettes to minimal stitching

---

## Target Audience

1. **The Modern Egyptian Woman (18–45)** — Values comfort, quality, and privacy in her intimate essentials
2. **The Confident Professional** — Wants premium everyday basics that perform under any outfit
3. **The Privacy-Conscious Shopper** — Prefers discreet online shopping over in-store experiences
4. **The Quality Seeker** — Chooses lasting comfort over fast-fashion trends
5. **The Gift Giver** — Looking for elegant, tastefully packaged intimate gifts

|-------|------|--------|

### Design Language

- **Rounded corners** (1.5rem radius) — soft, approachable, feminine
- **Generous white space** — clean, breathable layouts
- **Soft shadows & subtle borders** — refined depth without harshness
- **Imagery:** Lifestyle flat-lays, soft fabric close-ups, neutral-toned product shots, elegant minimalist compositions
- **Icons:** Lucide — clean, thin-line iconography

## Content Guidelines

- Always lead with comfort and confidence, not appearance
- Prioritize privacy and discretion in all messaging
- Use inclusive language — speak to all body types and ages
- Imagery should feel warm, intimate (not explicit), and aspirational
- Keep product descriptions focused on fabric quality, fit, and how it feels
- All content must be available in both English and Arabic
- Respect cultural sensitivities of the Egyptian market
